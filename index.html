About

Financial expertise,
built for founders and operators.

Synthwave Capital is a boutique financial advisory practice based in Texas, built for founders, operators, and leadership teams who need rigorous financial thinking without the complexity or cost of a full-time hire.

We specialize in the kind of work that actually moves companies forward, building models and providing services you will actually use. Whether you're raising your next round, managing board expectations, or simply trying to understand what's happening in your business, we bring the experience to help.

We have lived our professional lives in the venture-backed technology and product world with hands-on experience across Consumer, SaaS, and Fintech at every stage, from early seed through late-stage growth.
